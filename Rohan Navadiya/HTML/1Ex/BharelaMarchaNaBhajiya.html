Bharela Marcha Na Bhajiya Recipe

Bharela Marcha Na Bhajiya is a popular Gujarati snack made from stuffed green chilies dipped in gram flour batter and deep-fried until crispy. It's a spicy and delicious treat that pairs well with chai. Follow the recipe below to make your own Bharela Marcha Na Bhajiya!

Ingredients:

  • 10-12 large green chilies
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed, to make the batter
  • Oil, for deep frying

For the Stuffing:

  • 2-3 tablespoons roasted peanuts, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons desiccated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chilies: Slit the green chilies lengthwise and remove the seeds. Set aside.
  2. Make the Stuffing: In a bowl, mix together roasted peanuts, desiccated coconut, sesame seeds, chopped coriander leaves, lemon juice, and salt to taste. Stuff this mixture into the prepared green chilies.
  3. Prepare the Batter: In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, carom seeds, cumin seeds, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add water to make a smooth batter of coating consistency.
  4. Coat the Chilies: Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Dip each stuffed chili into the gram flour batter, ensuring it's evenly coated.
  5. Deep Fry: Carefully drop the coated chilies into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  6. Drain and Serve: Once done, remove the fried Bharela Marcha Na Bhajiya from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels. Serve hot with green chutney or sauce.
