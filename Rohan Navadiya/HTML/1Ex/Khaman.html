Khaman Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (plain)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon eno fruit salt
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • Grated coconut (optional, for garnish)
  • Chopped green chilies (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Batter:
    1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, semolina, yogurt, water, lemon juice, green chilies, ginger paste, sugar, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth batter. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Steam Khaman:
    1. Grease a steaming plate or tray with oil.
    2. Add eno fruit salt to the batter and mix gently. The batter will become frothy.
    3. Immediately pour the batter into the greased plate and spread it evenly.
    4. Steam the batter for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    5. Once steamed, remove the plate from the steamer and let it cool slightly.
  3. Prepare Tempering:
    1. Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
    2. Add sesame seeds and a pinch of asafoetida (hing). Saut√© for a few seconds until fragrant.
    3. Pour the tempering over the steamed khaman evenly.
  4. Cut and Garnish:
    1. Cut the steamed khaman into squares or diamonds using a knife.
    2. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves, grated coconut, and chopped green chilies if desired.
  5. Serve:
    1. Serve khaman warm with green chutney or tamarind chutney.
