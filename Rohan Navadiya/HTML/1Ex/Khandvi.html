Khandvi Recipe

Khandvi is a popular Indian snack made from gram flour (besan) and yogurt. It is a delicious and savory dish that originates from the state of Gujarat in India. Khandvi has a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture and is typically seasoned with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and grated coconut.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon green chili paste
  • Salt to taste
  • For tempering:
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
    • 4-5 curry leaves
    • 2 green chilies, chopped
    • 2 tablespoons grated coconut (optional)
    • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (coriander leaves) for garnish
